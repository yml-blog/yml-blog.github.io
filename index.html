About Me & Philosophy

I am a Product Manager, AI Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, and Data Scientist. I have experience in advanced data analysis and AI across five different industries, including:

  • Finance: BCIMC, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China
  • Healthcare: Provincial Health Authority, Island Health
  • Transportation/Public Safety: BC Public Service
  • Environment/Climate Change: Environment Canada and Climate Change
  • Education: University of Victoria, University of Waterloo, Wilfrid Laurier University

These roles span government (federal and provincial), NGOs, the private sector, and three distinct university research groups. Although there are differences in domain knowledge across so many industries, the core product and data thinking actually remain unchanged. From a first-principles perspective, everything ultimately returns to fundamental philosophy and methodology, with the only difference being the addition of domain-specific knowledge as a knowledge base. It's similar to how a large language model (LLM) can answer questions from any field once equipped with a specialized knowledge base.

I hope to use my blog to share some of the latest ideas and insights on data products. For me, it's also a way to document my growth in knowledge.
